Compound exercise for workout

Compound exercise is for building muscle and more on burning fat.Hence,instead of doing more isolate movements we should focus on more compound workout.



Example of Compound exercise

Isolation exercise means that you are only using one part of muscle during your workout. However it can build more muscle but not for burning fat.



Example of Isolation exercise





So which exercise should I do?

You need to do both to build muscle and burn fat.But a thing you can do is always put isolation workout at the end of your workout in order to make sure you can burn fat and build more muscle while you are in High Intensity.
